Create a premium single-page Electronic Press Kit website for the Rome-based DJ and producer “T0N1N0”.

TECH / OUTPUT
- Build with Next.js App Router, React, Tailwind CSS and Framer Motion.
- Keep the final page fast, responsive, accessible and deployable on Vercel.
- Use no color: only near-black, off-white, grey and opacity.
- Do not use generic gradients, neon colors, glass cards or stock nightclub clichés.
- Use a strong editorial grid, oversized typography, thin rules and restrained motion.

VISUAL IDENTITY
- Central motif: binary 0 and 1.
- Primary wordmark: T0N1N0.
- Tagline: “THERE IS NO MIDDLE STATE.”
- Mood: underground, intelligent, physical, high-fashion, technical, minimal.
